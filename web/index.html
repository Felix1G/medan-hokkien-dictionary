Medan Hokkien Dictionary

By Felix K. Sujono



A dictionary web-app of the Medan Hokkien dialect, which has linguistic roots to Penang Hokkien and is a subdialect of Zhang Zhou Hokkien. It is majoritively spoken by Chinese Indonesians in Medan and surrounding cities, including Chinese migrant communities in Jakarta. This dictionary captures the common and less common words of Medan Hokkien, including overlapping words from related languages, namely Penang Hokkien, Singapore Hokkien, and Taiwanese.

This dictionary hopes to preserve Medan Hokkien digitally while making it accessible for others who are curious of the language.

Disclaimer: This is a hobby project. I try my best to ensure accuracy, but this dictionary is not intended as a formal academic source. Many entries are referenced from Wiktionary, while others are my own contributions based on input from others. You are welcome to use this dictionary as a reference if you'd like.
