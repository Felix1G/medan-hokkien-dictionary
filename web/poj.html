POJ Quick Guide

A simple page to guide you how to read Pe̍h-oē-jī (POJ).

Forming a word in Hokkien is as such:.
You just need an [initial], then a [vowel], an optional [ending], and a tone.

[initial] + [vowel] + [ending] + [tone] = [Hokkien word]!



Below is a list of initials:

No. Initials IPA Matching English Equivalent Indonesian Equivalent 拼音对应音
1 m /m/ No difference. Sama persis. 同拼音。
2 n /n/ No difference. Sama persis. 同拼音。
3 ng /ŋ/ No difference. Though English does not have it as an initial. Sama persis. 没有区别，但在拼音中只能作韵尾。
4 p /p/ Think of it as the 'p' in 'spin', but not the 'p' in 'pin'. Sama persis. 像拼音的 ‘b’，比如 ‘不’。
5 ph /pʰ/ Just like English 'p' in 'pin', 'pet', and 'pour'. Huruf 'p' tapi lebih dihembus. 像拼音的 ‘p’，比如 ‘跑’。
6 t /t/ Think of it as the 't' in 'stop', but not the 't' in 'top'. Sama persis. 像拼音的 ‘d’，比如 ‘到’。
7 th /tʰ/ Just like English 't' in 'top', 'tea', and 'take'. Huruf 't' tapi lebih dihembus. 像拼音的 ‘t’，比如 ‘唐’。
8 k /k/ Think of it as the 'k' in 'skate', but not the 'k'-sound as in 'cat'. Sama persis. 像拼音的 ‘g’，比如 ‘高’。
9 kh /kʰ/ Just like English 'c' or 'k' in 'cat', 'car', and 'king'. Huruf 'k' tapi lebih dihembus. 像拼音的 ‘k’，比如 ‘卡’。
10 b /b/ No difference. Sama persis. 中文没有。
11 g /g/ No difference. Sama persis. 中文没有。
12 ch /t͡s/ No equivalent. Think of it as the 'ts' in 'cats'. Hampir sama dengan huruf 'c'. 像拼音的 ‘z’，比如 ‘资’。
13 chi /t͡ɕ/ No equivalent. Closest would be the 'ch' in 'cheese', but softer and more fronted. Seperti huruf 'c' tapi lebih lembut. 像拼音的 ‘j’，比如 ‘鸡’。
14 chh /t͡sʰ/ No equivalent. Think of it as the 'ts' in 'cats', but with a strong puff of air. Seperti huruf 'c' tapi dihembus. 像拼音的 ‘c’，比如 ‘次’。
15 chhi /t͡ɕʰ/ No equivalent. Closest would be the 'ch' in 'cheese', but softer, more fronted, and with puff of air. Seperti huruf 'c' tapi dihembus dan lebih lembut. 像拼音的 ‘q’，比如 ‘七’。
16 j /d͡z/ No equivalent. Closest would be the 'ds' in 'kids' or 'ads'. Seperti huruf 't' dan 'j' digabung menjadi 'tj' (bukan ejaan lama 'c'). 中文没有。
17 ji /d͡ʑ/ Like the ‘j’ in 'jam', but softer and a little more like ‘y’. Hampir sama dengan huruf 'j'. 中文没有。
18 s /s/ No difference. Sama persis. 同拼音。
19 si /ɕ/ Like ‘sh’ in 'she', but softer and more fronted. Hampir sama dengan 's' tapi lebih lembut dan di depan mulut. 像拼音的 ‘x’，比如 ‘西’。
20 h /h/ No difference. Sama persis. 同拼音。
21 l /l/ * No difference. Sama persis. 同拼音。
* Might be /ɭ/ in other regions, however due to Indo/Malay influence, the sound in Medan Hokkien is /l/.

Below is a list of vowels:

No. Vowels IPA Matching English Equivalent Indonesian Equivalent 拼音对应音
1 i /i/ Like 'ee' in 'keen', 'teen', and 'bee'. Sama persis. 发‘i’的音，就像‘衣’。
2 iⁿ /ĩ/ Just like No. 1, but breath out your nose at the same time. Seperti No. 1 sambil napas hidung keluar. 像No. 1，但是鼻子也要出气。
3 e /e/ Similar to 'ay' in 'say' and 'bay' but without a glide. Huruf 'e' antara 'besar' dan 'enak'. (EEEE A) 发‘e’的音，就像‘飞’，但不要加‘i’。
4 eⁿ /ɛ/ Like 'e' in 'bed' or 'get'. Huruf 'e' dalam kata 'enak'. 发‘e’的音，就像‘写’，但不要加‘i’。
5 a /a/ Like 'a' in 'father' while opening your mouth wide. Sama persis. 就像‘啊’。
6 aⁿ /ã/ Just like No. 5, but breath out your nose at the same time. Seperti No. 5 sambil napas hidung keluar. 像No. 5，但是鼻子也要出气。
7 u /u/ Like 'oo' in 'foot' or 'boot'. Sama persis. 发‘u’的音，就像‘五’。
8 uⁿ /ũ/ Just like No. 7, but breath out your nose at the same time. Seperti No. 7 sambil napas hidung keluar. 像No. 7，但是鼻子也要出气。
9 o /o/ Like 'o' in 'go' without a glide. Huruf 'o' dalam kata 'toko'. 发‘o’的音，嘴唇微微圆。
10 o͘ /ɔ/ Like 'o' in 'long', 'song', and 'cross'. Huruf 'o' dalam kata 'longgar' atau 'sedot'. 发‘o’的音，就像‘我’。
11 oⁿ /ɔ̃/ Just like No. 10, but breath out your nose at the same time. Seperti No. 10 sambil napas hidung keluar. 像No. 10，但是鼻子也要出气。
12 eʳ /ə/ Like 'a' in 'about', the sound of 'uh' Huruf 'e' dalam kata 'besar'. 发‘e’的音，就像‘的’。
13 ir /ɨ/ In between 'i' from No. 1 and 'eʳ' from No. 12. Di antara 'i' dari No. 1 dan 'eʳ' dari No. 12. 发‘i’的音，就像‘子’。
Vowels like eⁿ and o might be different, and that the schwa (eʳ) isnt usually in Standard POJ. Hokkien in the SEA (Southeast Asia) region has slightly different vowels but correspond well to POJ.

Below is the list of endings:

No. Endings IPA Matching English Equivalent Indonesian Equivalent
1 -m /m/ or /m̩/ Like 'm' in 'mom' and 'come'. Bunyi 'm'.
2 -n /n/ Like 'n' in 'bun' and 'shown'. Bunyi 'n'.
3 -ng /ŋ/ or /ŋ̍/ Like 'ng' in 'song' and 'boing'. Bunyi 'ng'.
4 -p /p̚/ Like 'p' in 'lap' and 'gap', just closing the lips. Bunyi akhiran 'p'.
5 -t /t̚/ Like 't' in 'bat' and 'cat', just biting the tip of the tounge. Bunyi akhiran 't'.
6 -k /k̚/ Like 'k' in 'back' and 'deck', without producing a 'k' sound. Bunyi akhiran 'k'.
7 -h /ʔ/ Say 'uh-oh', the pause in between is the glottal stop. Seperti tahan napas sebentar di tenggorokan, contohnya 'tidak'.

No language is tougher or easier, this includes Hokkien.
In addition to all the above, a tone must be assigned.
There are 7 tones in Medan Hokkien.
It is important to note that Hokkien has extensive tone sandhi (tone changes depending on what tone is before the word). Therefore, tones should not be pronounced exactly in words or sentences.
Below is a list of tones:

Tone Number Tone Markings Graph Explanation
1 a (no symbol) High flat tone.
2 á High falling tone. (high -> mid)
3 à Low falling tone. (mid -> low)
4 ah, ak, at, ap (ending in h, k, t, or p) Falling tone, short vowel (high -> low)
5 â Rising tone (low -> high)
7 ā Mid flat tone.
8 a̍h, a̍k, a̍p, a̍t (ending in h, k, t, or, p) Rising tone, short vowel (mid low -> mid high)
Tone 6 used to exist historically but as long since merged with either tone 7 or tone 2.
