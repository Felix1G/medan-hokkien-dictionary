Notes:
1. Order of definitions will be the same.
> example: [advb A, adj B] will remain as adverb first then adjective second.
2. Definitions may be swapped to be grouped to the categories, but initial category order will be the same.
> example: [advb A, adj B, advb C] => [advb A, advb C, adj B] B and C will be swapped to group adverbs into one category.
3. Hashtag (#) must never be part of the definition.
> Hashtag is used as category specifiers in definitionDisplay.
4. Hanzi and POJ must not have any numbers (0 - 9) on them.
> Numbers may mess up with POJ tone searching
